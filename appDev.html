My Html experience

My journey into HTML began with a curiosity about how websites are built. I was always fascinated by the seamless functionality and beautiful designs of various websites I visited. This curiosity led me to explore the foundational technologies behind web

I started with online tutorials and free resources, such as W3Schools and MDN Web Docs, which provided a solid grounding in HTML. These platforms offered interactive examples and exercises, making the learning process engaging and practical.

To deepen my understanding, I enrolled in a beginner-friendly online course on a platform like FreeCodeCamp (Certificate is in my LinkedIn Profile). The course was instrumental in teaching me the basics of HTML, including elements, tags, attributes, and the structure of a web page.

Additionally, I engaged in hands-on practice by creating simple web pages and small projects. This practical approach helped me to solidify my knowledge and gain confidence in my abilities.

Over time, I expanded my learning to include CSS and JavaScript, enabling me to build more dynamic and visually appealing websites. This comprehensive understanding of web development has been a rewarding journey, and I continue to refine my skills and stay updated with the latest trends and best practices in the industry.
