My JavaScript experience

My journey into JavaScript began after gaining a solid foundation in HTML and CSS. While these technologies allowed me to structure and style web pages, I wanted to add interactivity and dynamic functionality to my websites. This curiosity led me to explore JavaScript, the powerful scripting language of the web.

I started with online tutorials and free resources such as W3Schools and MDN Web Docs. These platforms provided a thorough introduction to JavaScript basics, including variables, data types, functions, and event handling. The interactive examples and exercises made the learning process engaging and practical.

To deepen my knowledge, I enrolled in beginner-friendly online course on a platform such as FreeCodeCamp (Certificate is in my LinkedIn Profile).

However, I recognize that I have trouble to understand and fully solidified my knowledge of JavaScript. I continue to dedicate time to learning and practicing these topics, knowing that mastery comes with persistence and continuous effort.

My journey with JavaScript is ongoing, and I am committed to refining my skills and staying updated with the latest developments in the language. I am excited to continue learning and improving, ultimately aiming to create more dynamic and interactive web experiences.
